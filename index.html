HUMANITARIAN MISSION: MISSING PERSONS SUPPORT

BESA VENTURES leverages the blockchain to fund and support humanitarian initiatives focused on missing persons and family reunification. Through NFT drops, donation drives, and charity auctions, a portion of ecosystem proceeds goes directly to vetted organizations facilitating searches, awareness campaigns, and victim support.

How crypto & NFTs help

  • Transparent funds: on-chain allocations ensure traceability
  • Targeted campaigns: NFT sales and charity auctions fund specific cases
  • Community awareness: players amplify missing persons cases through in-game events

Donation Call-to-Action

Support search and family reunification efforts. Donations are audited and a public ledger of allocations is published quarterly.
